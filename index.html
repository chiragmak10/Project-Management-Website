About Rit Library

The RIT Central Library with its state-of-the-art building, hardware, software and other allied resources is fast developing into one of the finest institutional libraries in Western Maharashtra. Its inviting architecture provides an ambiance that is relaxed but modern; and space that is large yet intimate. The resources available include books on an impressive array of subjects, course-specific reading material, academic journals and various digital resources. It houses sufficient reference and reading material for students and faculty from multiple disciplines

RIT central library has a good collection of 96225 + volumes, 30771 + titles as well as 6524 e-books, of all engineering disciplines and management department, includes more than 8313+ reference books, 416 Standards. It includes extracurricular and competitive examination books such as GATE, TOFEL, and MPSC etc. Its total investment of Rs. 4.41 crores.

The Central Library invested 5.26 lakhs for subscribing more than 167 print journals of engineering and management.

It is also subscribed ASCE, Science Direct, IEEE(ASPP+POP), IET, SAE, Delnet, Knimbus, and J-Gate & EBESCO.

E-Journals databases. Its subscription cost is more than 29 lakhs
